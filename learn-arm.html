ARM Components Explained

Index

The index is a benchmark interest rate that reflects market conditions. Adjustable-rate mortgages use the 30 Day Average SOFR index to set future rates.

Margin

The margin is a fixed percentage added to the index to determine your interest rate at each adjustment.

Caps

Caps limit how much your interest rate can rise. They apply to the first adjustment, subsequent adjustments, and over the life of the loan.

Change Dates

Your rate adjusts on scheduled change dates. After the initial fixed period, adjustments occur every subsequent period defined by the ARM product.
