Program Snapshot

Equip yourself to advise clients on BCSB's construction-to-permanent solution. Core program elements include:

  • Finance up to 90% loan-to-value (LTV) using the lower of total acquisition cost (lot + construction) or the appraised value.
  • Single close structure with 12 months of interest-only payments on funds disbursed for construction.
  • Automatic conversion to a fully amortizing loan for up to a 30-year term after the project reaches completion.
  • Initial closing disbursement is calculated as Loan Amount − Build Price; the construction budget is managed through future draws.
One-time close workflow
  1. Closing: The entire construction-to-permanent loan is documented at once. Funds beyond the construction budget release at the table.
  2. Construction phase (up to 12 months): Draws are distributed as inspections confirm progress. The borrower remits interest-only on dollars advanced.
  3. Conversion: When the home is complete, the note automatically transitions to long-term amortization with no second closing required.
