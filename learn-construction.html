Program Snapshot

Our single-close construction-to-permanent loan keeps your project on track from groundbreaking through move-in. Key program features include:

  • Finance up to 90% loan-to-value (LTV) using the lower of total acquisition cost (lot + construction) or the appraised value.
  • One-time closing with 12 months of interest-only payments on funds disbursed during construction.
  • Automatic conversion to a fully amortizing loan for up to a 30-year term once the home is complete.
  • Initial closing disbursement is calculated as Loan Amount − Build Price; the construction budget is held for future draws.

How the One-Time Close Works
  1. Closing: We approve the full construction-to-permanent loan. Any funds beyond the construction budget are disbursed at closing.
  2. Construction phase (up to 12 months): We release construction draws as work is completed. You pay interest-only on the funds that have been disbursed.
  3. Completion: Once the home is finished, the loan seamlessly converts to a permanent mortgage with up to a 30-year amortization.
