Program Snapshot

BCSB's construction-to-permanent loan lets your clients finance the lot, the build, and the permanent mortgage in a single closing. Here are the key terms at a glance:

Up to 90% LTV
Based on the lower of total acquisition cost (lot + construction) or the appraised value.
Single Close
One closing covers both the construction and permanent financing — no second closing required.
12 Months Interest-Only
During construction, clients pay interest only on the funds actually disbursed.
Up to 30-Year Term
Automatically converts to a fully amortizing loan once the project is complete.
The initial disbursement at closing equals Loan Amount − Build Price. The rest of the construction budget is released to the builder through future draws.

How the one-time close works

  1. Closing
    The entire construction-to-permanent loan is documented at once. Funds beyond the construction budget release at the table.
  2. Construction phase (up to 12 months)
    Draws are distributed as inspections confirm progress. The borrower pays interest only on the dollars advanced.
  3. Conversion
    When the home is complete, the note automatically transitions to long-term amortization — no second closing required.
